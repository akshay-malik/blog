0.0.3 Why study transportation systems as data systems?

Having worked for the City for over two years, I have had the pleasure and priveledge of working with a group of hard working and talented government employees. This time and interaction has allowed me to see that lack of resources has caused several people hours to go waste in doing tasks that should clearly be automated. This situation encouraged me to start this running list of systems that the City needs desperately. Some of them have been identified and their procurement is just stuck in some red-tape stuff. Others are yet to be identified or have political issues as serious barriers to moving forward. So here it is:
