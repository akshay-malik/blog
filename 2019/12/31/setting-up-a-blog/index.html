Why make a blog

This question is reasonable as there are several options such as Medium or Wordpress that can help one get setup with a blog without much trouble. The reasons I decided to setup my own blog are as follows:

  1. Show off R skills - I wanted to have a blog specifically to show off data science work. Setting up a blog using Rstudio provides me with a straight forward R project to get started on.
  2. Rmarkdown is much better at presenting analytical work than markdown (Jekyll).
  3. Efficient Workflow - Having a work flow setup that allows publishing directly from Rstudio allows is very convinient and allows me to focus on content once I have everything setup.
  4. Design - Being a trained architect, I cannot work on a system that does not allow me full control of how it looks. May be a bit of OCD here but that’s just me.
